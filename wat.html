About Wat Chetawan

Wat Tham Chetawan is a remarkable Buddhist temple located in Na Noi District, Nan Province, Thailand. Once a neglected and overgrown site, this sacred place has been transformed into one of the most important Buddhist sites in the region.

The temple is deeply connected to the legacy of Kruba Noi Yanwichai, the revered monk known as the "Little Saint" who dedicated his life to spiritual practice and transformed this sacred space through his devotion and ascetic practices.

The temple features a unique fusion of Lanna (Northern Thai) and Burmese architectural styles, reflecting the deep spiritual and cultural connections of the region.
